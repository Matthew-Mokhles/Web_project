Certainly! Here are eight English books along with their cover photos, names, and descriptions:

1. **"To Kill a Mockingbird" by Harper Lee**
   
   *Description:* Harper Lee's timeless classic explores racial injustice and moral growth through the eyes of a young girl in the American South during the 1930s.

2. **"1984" by George Orwell**
   
   *Description:* Orwell's dystopian masterpiece depicts a totalitarian regime where individuality is suppressed, and reality is controlled by the government's omnipresent surveillance.

3. **"The Great Gatsby" by F. Scott Fitzgerald**
   
   *Description:* Set in the Jazz Age, Fitzgerald's novel delves into the glamorous yet corrupt lives of the wealthy, exploring themes of love, wealth, and the American Dream.

4. **"Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone" by J.K. Rowling**
   
   *Description:* Rowling's magical tale follows the journey of a young wizard, Harry Potter, as he discovers his heritage and confronts the dark wizard who killed his parents.

5. **"Pride and Prejudice" by Jane Austen**
   
   *Description:* Austen's romantic comedy explores societal expectations, marriage, and love through the spirited Elizabeth Bennet and the proud Mr. Darcy.

6. **"The Catcher in the Rye" by J.D. Salinger**
   
   *Description:* Salinger's novel follows Holden Caulfield, a disenchanted teenager, as he navigates the complexities of adolescence and searches for authenticity in a world he finds phony.

7. **"The Hobbit" by J.R.R. Tolkien**
   
   *Description:* Tolkien's fantasy adventure introduces readers to the world of Middle-earth, following the journey of Bilbo Baggins as he embarks on a quest to reclaim treasure guarded by the dragon Smaug.

8. "The Silent Patient" by Alex Michaelides

Description: This gripping psychological thriller revolves around Alicia Berenson, a famous painter who inexplicably murders her husband and then stops speaking. Theo Faber, a psychotherapist, becomes obsessed with uncovering the truth behind her silence and delves deep into her past, unraveling shocking secrets along the way. "The Silent Patient" is a page-turner filled with twists and turns that keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.
